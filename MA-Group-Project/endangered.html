Endangered Species

What are Endangered Species?

Endangered species are species which are close to dying out, this could be from a variety
of reasons but like most have to do with the environment for example habitat loss and
water pollution affecting resources or Invasive species which can spread sickness, take
resources, and could also hunt these dying species.

Why is a Species being Endangered bad?

A species close to dying out means that it is close to dying out and that the species will
be lost forever, lowing bio-diversity and as most of these extinction are through artificial
means their extinction can through the ecosystem out of balance and cause many other population
to have reduced size which would became endangered and possible also extinct.

There are many things that can be done but actions like preventing the transference of organisms

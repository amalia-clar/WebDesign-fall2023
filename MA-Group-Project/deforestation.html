What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the action of clearing a wide area of trees. Deforestation usually takes place for the purpose of creating more farm land. Deforestation is bad because it completely clears out trees and puts something in its place. Forest loss and damage is the cause of 10% of global warming, which is why it's so necessary to work to stop deforestation.

What can you do to help?

  • Consuming less
  • Avoiding single-use plastics
  • Eating sustainable food
  • Recycling
